Nuwara Eliya farmers

At Green Store, we take great pride in our commitment to supporting local farmers and promoting sustainable agriculture. Our partnership with Nuwara Eliya farmers allows us to bring you the freshest and highest quality vegetables straight from the heart of Sri Lanka's hill country. Nestled amidst the breathtaking landscapes and cool climate of Nuwara Eliya, these farmers cultivate their produce with care and dedication, ensuring each vegetable is packed with flavor and nutrients

By sourcing directly from Nuwara Eliya farmers, we not only guarantee the freshness of our vegetables but also support the livelihoods of local farming communities. We prioritize ethical sourcing practices and work closely with farmers who share our values of sustainability and environmental stewardship.

When you choose Green Store, you're not just buying vegetables; you're investing in the future of local agriculture and contributing to the preservation of traditional farming practices in Nuwara Eliya. Join us in celebrating the rich agricultural heritage of Sri Lanka while enjoying the freshest and most delicious vegetables nature has to offer

Badulla Fresh Vegetables Market

At Green Store, we are passionate about sourcing the finest quality vegetables while supporting local farmers and communities. Our partnership with the Badulla Fresh Vegetables Market allows us to bring you a diverse selection of fresh, locally grown produce straight from the fertile lands of the Uva region.

Nestled in the heart of Badulla, surrounded by picturesque landscapes and lush valleys, the Badulla Fresh Vegetables Market is a bustling hub of agricultural activity. Here, dedicated farmers bring their harvests to market, showcasing the bounty of the region's rich soils and favorable climate.

Peradeniya All Fresh Vegetables Farmers Community

At Green Store, we are dedicated to providing our customers with the freshest and highest quality vegetables while supporting local farming communities. Our partnership with the Peradeniya All Fresh Farmers Community allows us to offer a wide variety of locally sourced produce straight from the fields of the Kandy district.

By sourcing directly from the Peradeniya All Fresh Farmers Community, we ensure that you receive vegetables of exceptional freshness and flavor. Our close relationship with the farming community enables us to prioritize ethical sourcing practices and support small-scale farmers.

Situated near the scenic town of Peradeniya, the Peradeniya All Fresh Farmers Community is comprised of passionate farmers who cultivate their produce with care and dedication. These farmers are committed to sustainable farming practices and preserving the rich agricultural heritage of the region.
