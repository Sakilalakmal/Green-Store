Loyalty Programmes

Green Points Rewards

  • Customers earn points for every purchase they make at Green Store Grocery.
  • These points can be redeemed for discounts on future purchases or for eco-friendly products available at the store.
  • Bonus points can be awarded for purchasing environmentally friendly products or for bringing their own reusable bags.
  • Customers can track their points easily through an app or a dedicated online portal.

Tree Planting Initiative

  • For every certain amount spent, Green Store Grocery pledges to plant a tree.
  • Customers can track the number of trees they've contributed to planting through their loyalty account.
  • Additionally, customers who reach a certain spending threshold within a set period could have a tree planted in their name.
  • This program not only incentivizes customer loyalty but also promotes environmental sustainability.

Reusable Program

  • Green Store Grocery encourages customers to bring their own reusable containers for bulk items.
  • For every reusable container used, customers earn a stamp or digital token.
  • After collecting a certain number of stamps or tokens, customers are rewarded with a free product or a discount on their next purchase.
  • This program not only reduces waste but also fosters customer loyalty by incentivizing sustainable shopping practices.
